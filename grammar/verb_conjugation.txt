Present tense verbs currently always end with a consonant. Their conjugation is
currently dependent on the subject that performs the action.

sgl.
1st stem + ù
2nd stem + ash
3rd stem + ötfh

plr.
1st stem + ùp
2nd stem + ashp
3rd	stem + ötfhp

As you can see, the Plural form is always formed with according to
singular form + p
